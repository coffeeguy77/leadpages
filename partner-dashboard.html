About partner templates

A template saves your preferred app layout — which sections are on, in what order — so you can apply it to any new site with one click.

To save a template: open any site in the editor, go to the App Marketplace tab, enable the apps you want, then click "Save current layout as template".

To apply a template: open the target site in the editor, go to the App Marketplace tab, click "Apply a template to this site" and pick from your saved list.
