Questions

The things people ask first.

Does it cost me anything?

No. In almost every case the lender pays the broker when your loan settles, so our service is free to you. If a product ever carries a fee, we tell you before you commit to anything.

How many lenders do you actually compare?

More than 30 — the big four, plus mutuals and specialist lenders. We match the structure to your situation rather than chasing the lowest headline rate, which often hides fees.

I'm self-employed. Can you still help?

Yes. We know which lenders take a sensible view of business and self-employed income, and how to present your numbers so you're assessed fairly.

Is my current rate still any good?

Probably worth a look. Lenders keep their best rates for new customers, so existing borrowers quietly drift onto higher ones. A free review tells you in minutes whether you're overpaying.
