# LeadPages Tracking And Analytics

LeadPages tracks visitor behaviour, leads, and site events.

## Key Concepts

- Public site events
- Lead form submissions
- Analytics dashboard
- Stats API
- UTM tracking
- Conversion tracking

## Rules

- Lead capture must be reliable.
- Public endpoints should not fail visibly to visitors.
- Tracking should not slow down public pages.
- Analytics must remain tenant-specific.
- Do not expose one tenant's data to another tenant.

## Future Ideas

- Better conversion dashboards
- Partner-level rollups
- Client-level lead quality scoring
- AI lead summaries
- Email/SMS follow-up automation
