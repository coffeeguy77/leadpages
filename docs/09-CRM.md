# LeadPages CRM And Leads

LeadPages captures leads from tenant websites and stores them for the site owner/partner.

## Core Concepts

- Lead capture forms
- Lead notifications
- CRM dashboard
- Email opt-outs
- Events associated with leads
- Partner/client visibility

## Lead Capture Rules

- Never lose a lead because of a non-critical backend failure.
- Email notification failure should not block lead storage.
- Tenant isolation is critical.
- Form spam and abuse should be considered.
