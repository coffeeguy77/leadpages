# LeadPages Partner System

The partner system allows agencies or resellers to create and manage client sites.

## Business Rules

- Partners can generate clients for LeadPages.
- Clients may see the partner as their primary support contact.
- LeadPages remains the platform owner in the background.
- Partner/client relationships may need to be transferred.
- Commission and recurring revenue logic must be auditable.

## Core Partner Needs

- Partner login
- Partner dashboard
- Client list
- Client creation
- Partner profile/homepage
- Support ownership
- Commission reporting
- Training resources
- Account transfer process

## AI Rules

Do not change partner ownership logic without approval.

Before editing partner functionality, document:

- Which tables are used
- How active partner status is checked
- Which client/site records are affected
- Whether the change affects commission, support, or ownership
