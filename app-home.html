LeadPages

LeadPages is a website and landing-page platform for Australian small businesses and agency partners. Use the App Command Centre to publish sites, manage landing pages, capture leads, and connect marketing tools.

What LeadPages does

LeadPages helps you design, publish, and manage customer websites and landing pages from one workspace. Partners and site owners can edit content, themes, forms, SEO settings, and publishing without writing code.

Google account access: When you connect Google Ads, LeadPages requests access so you can link your Google Ads account, sync campaign and account performance into your site workspace, and upload website conversion events that help measure lead outcomes. LeadPages does not use Google user data to sell ads or share it with unrelated third parties. See the Privacy Policy for details on access, use, storage, and retention.
