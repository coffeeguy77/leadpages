LeadPages by Web Culture is an Australian website and landing-page platform for small businesses and agency partners. Use the App Command Centre to publish sites, manage landing pages, capture leads, and connect marketing tools.

Purpose of this application

LeadPages by Web Culture helps partners and site owners design, publish, and manage customer websites and landing pages from one workspace — including content, themes, forms, SEO settings, and publishing — without writing code.

This application homepage describes the LeadPages product operated by Web Culture (Bean Culture Pty Ltd). It is the application users authorize when connecting Google services from the App Command Centre at app.leadpages.com.au.

How LeadPages by Web Culture uses Google user data

When you connect Google Ads, LeadPages by Web Culture requests access so you can link your Google Ads account, sync campaign and account performance into your site workspace, and upload website conversion events that help measure lead outcomes.

We do not use Google user data to sell ads, and we do not share it with unrelated third parties. See the Privacy Policy for access, use, storage, and retention details.
