Common questions

Asked by almost everyone

Do I need any technical or design experience?
No. Every site starts from a professionally designed, industry-ready theme with the copy already written. Your job is swapping in the client’s details, photos and colours — the editor is built for non-technical people, and AI helps write anything custom.
What does LeadPages cost me?
Your platform costs are a flat, predictable amount per site — covered in the demo, where we’ll walk through the numbers against what you plan to charge. There are no revenue splits: what you invoice clients is yours.
How do I find clients?
Locally, mostly. Businesses without websites (or with embarrassing ones) are everywhere — we provide outreach templates, a portfolio of demo sites to show, and the marketplace pages to send prospects. Plus business owners who enquire through our Find-a-Partner page get matched to partners near them.
How much time does it take?
Building a site takes hours, not weeks. Most partners run this in school hours or evenings; a handful of care-plan clients needs only a few hours a month.
Who owns the client relationship?
You do. Your brand, your invoices, your prices, your clients. LeadPages is your supplier, not your middleman.
